IntraVybe Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to IntraVybe! IntraVybe ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our social media app ("App") and services (collectively, "Services"). By using IntraVybe, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

  • 1.1 Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email, phone number, username, and password.
  • 1.2 Profile Information: Additional optional info like profile picture, bio, location, and social media links.
  • 1.3 Content: Text posts, photos, videos, comments, and shared materials.
  • 1.4 Communication: Messages and interactions on the app.

2. Information We Collect Automatically

  • 2.1 Usage Information: Feature usage, viewed content, timestamps.
  • 2.2 Device Information: Device type, OS, identifiers, network info, IP address.
  • 2.3 Location Information: Collected with your consent using GPS, Wi-Fi, etc.

3. Information from Third Parties

We may receive your information from third-party platforms if you link accounts.

How We Use Your Information

  • 1. To Provide and Improve Services: Deliver core features, personalize experiences, analyze usage, develop new tools.
  • 2. To Communicate: Notifications, updates, support, and optional marketing messages.
  • 3. To Ensure Safety and Security: Detect fraud, enforce policies, comply with laws.
  • 4. To Show Relevant Ads: Personalized advertising (with opt-out options).

How We Share Your Information

  • 1. With Other Users: Shared content and profile info is visible to others.
  • 2. With Third-Party Providers: Hosting, analytics, payment, and support vendors under confidentiality agreements.
  • 3. With Business Partners: Co-marketing or features (only with your consent).
  • 4. For Legal Reasons: Compliance with law enforcement or legal orders.
  • 5. In Business Transfers: During mergers, acquisitions, or sales, with prior notice.

Your Choices and Rights

  • 1. Access & Update: Modify your profile info in the App or contact us.
  • 2. Delete Account: Available through app settings; backups may retain data briefly.
  • 3. Marketing Opt-Out: Use unsubscribe links or contact us.
  • 4. Control Location: Adjust device settings to revoke location permissions.
  • 5. Manage Tracking: Use browser settings to control cookies.

Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data, but no method is 100% secure.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, under protective agreements where needed.

Children's Privacy

IntraVybe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data, and will delete it if found.
