Community Guidelines

Welcome to our community! These guidelines are designed to foster a safe, respectful, and enjoyable environment for all users. By using our app, you agree to follow these rules and encourage others to do the same.

1. Respect Others

  • Treat all users with kindness, respect, and dignity.
  • No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discriminatory behavior is allowed.
  • Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation will not be tolerated.

2. Appropriate Content

  • Do not share content that contains nudity, pornography, or sexually explicit material.
  • Violent, graphic, or gory imagery is strictly prohibited (except for educational or news reporting contexts).
  • No promotion or glorification of self-harm, suicide, or dangerous behavior.

3. Language and Communication

  • Use appropriate language when posting, commenting, or messaging.
  • Strong, abusive, or offensive language is not permitted.
  • Encourage constructive discussions, not arguments or hostility.

4. Substance Use and Gambling

  • No promotion, sale, or encouragement of illegal drugs or controlled substances.
  • No simulated or real-money gambling unless explicitly allowed by law and platform policies.
  • Do not glorify excessive alcohol consumption or unsafe use of substances.

5. User-Generated Content

  • Ensure any content you upload (text, images, videos, audio) is your own or you have rights to share it.
  • Do not infringe on others’ intellectual property rights (copyright, trademarks, etc.).
  • Content that violates laws or regulations will be removed.

6. Privacy and Safety

  • Do not share your own or others’ private information (addresses, phone numbers, etc.).
  • Never impersonate another person or misrepresent your identity.
  • Report suspicious, abusive, or harmful behavior immediately.

7. Community and Collaboration

  • Encourage creativity, learning, and positive interactions.
  • Give constructive feedback when engaging with others’ content.
  • Help maintain a welcoming space for all backgrounds, ages, and cultures.

8. Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in content removal, warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent account bans. We reserve the right to enforce these guidelines at our discretion to maintain community safety.

9. Updates to Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines from time to time to reflect changes in our app, policies, or applicable laws. Please check back regularly to stay informed.

By using our app, you acknowledge and agree to abide by these Community Guidelines.
